# 📊 B2B Sales Monitoring Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The B2B Sales Monitoring Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, client engagement, and regional sales trends. It enables businesses to monitor key sales metrics, identify top-performing clients and sales representatives, and make data-driven decisions.
## 🎯 Objectives
- Track overall sales and profit performance.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Analyze revenue by product category.
- Evaluate sales performance by region.
- Identify top clients and sales representatives.
- Measure target achievement and profit margins.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Tableau
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence (BI)

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Sales:** ₹15.28M+
- **Total Profit:** ₹3.21M+
- **Profit Margin:** 22.94%
- **Client Count:** 177
- **Target Achievement:** 53.45%

## 📊 Dashboard Features
### 1. Sales Overview
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Client Count
- Target Achievement

### 2. Monthly Sales Trend
- Month-wise sales analysis
- Seasonal sales performance tracking

### 3. Industry-wise Revenue Analysis
- Revenue contribution by product categories:
  - Hardware
  - Services
  - Software

### 4. Top Client Analysis
- Top 10 clients by sales revenue
- Customer contribution insights

### 5. Regional Sales Performance
- Sales comparison across:
  - North
  - South
  - East
  - West

### 6. Sales Representative Performance
- Individual sales representative performance tracking
- Top performer identification

### 7. Category-wise Sales Distribution
- Product category sales contribution visualization
